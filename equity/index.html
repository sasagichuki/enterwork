EQUITY CEO’S CHRISTMAS MESSAGE

In 2013, Kenya celebrated 50 years of independence and in true patriotic fashion, celebrated an unforgettable Golden Jubilee. Equity Bank of Kenya being an original product of the country, decided to share this monumental occasion with their customers by sharing a special message with a twist.

The Problem

With so many banks communicating their season’s greetings to their customers in the same old style and manner, Equity needed to be different especially during this momentous occasion.

The Insight

Over 85% of the Kenyan population have access to mobile phones. As such it is the best communication medium to reach the highest number of people at a given time.

Our Idea

Being the season of festivities, we helped the CEO of Equity Bank give his customers an unforgettable surprise. He shared his special festive message to customers across the country through a unique video communication that had a personalised SMS surprise midway through.

How we did it

  • Step 1

    Equity Bank customers would receive an EDM with an invitation from the CEO to view their special message from him.

  • Step 2

    Upon clicking, the customer would go to a specially designed landing page that featured a video of the CEO delivering his message of hope and jubilation.

  • Step 3

    Mid-way through the video, the CEO would take out his mobile phone and type a text message. At that exact moment the user would receive a personalised SMS from the CEO marking the country’s 50-year milestone as well as wishing them joyous returns during the festive season.

  • Step 4

    Go on, give it a try and experience this fun surprise that put a smile across the faces of thousands of Kenyans over the festive season.
