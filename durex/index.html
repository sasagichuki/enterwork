PAY WITH A DUREX

Durex is the world’s number 1 condom brand. When Valentine’s season came around, they knew they had to stand out from other brands who were fighting for consumer attention during this prime time, and they did. They took an innovative lead in the market, one that had never been seen before, transforming their product into a valuable currency that made Valentine’s day a very memorable day for many.

The Problem

Durex needed to overcome Kenyan’s general stigma towards buying condoms and stand out from other brand promotions, offers and advertising during this Valentine’s season.

The Insight

Every year couples try to make Valentine’s Day as passionate and as romantic as possible, consequently raising the question – what to do?

Our Idea

We gave fans the chance to have the best Valentine's day ever. We took everyone on the ultimate love adventure that had them racing across the city to be the 5 lucky couples to wine and dine in the city’s finest, exclusive 5 star restaurants on Valentine’s day. Best part was, to indulge in this unforgettable Valentine’s treat, all they had to do was Pay for it with a Durex.

How we did it

  • Step 1

    Fans would spot the ‘#PaysToCome1st posts on Facebook and Twitter for details on where to go to trade-in their condoms for their dinner reward.

  • Step 2

    They would rush to the nearest retail outlet and buy 2 condom packs

  • Step 3

    Just like that, the 5 fastest couples found themselves wining and dining in the city’s finest, exclusive 5 star restaurants on Valentine’s day, and all they had to do was pay for this unforgettable experience with their Durex condoms.
